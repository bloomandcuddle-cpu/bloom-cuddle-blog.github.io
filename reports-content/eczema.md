## ü§î Eczema vs. Dry Skin

**Baby Eczema:**
- Red, inflamed patches
- Intense itching
- May have small bumps or blisters
- Often appears on cheeks, elbows, knees

**Dry Skin:**
- Rough, flaky texture
- Mild itching
- No inflammation
- Can appear anywhere
